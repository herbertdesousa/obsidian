Instead of give excuse to the TL or something is hard, give them alternative to do something different.

- The code is a shit? suggest how good is refactoring
- The time is short? suggest how can it be broken in small deliveries

Also, prevent expected questions by thinking "what they'll going to say?", including all of this in your proposal.
