Techniques to good communications

#### Focus on topic
 for texts or talks do: **Write in bullets** and get them across the text

#### Know your audience
Do not talk technically too much to a product person
- What your audience will learn with you?
- What are they interest?
- How technically good are they?
- How can you make them engaged 

#### Choose a good moment
Control your anxiety, Do not talk in a 6pm Friday

#### Choose a style
Be formal or verbose, if you don't know, **ask them**

#### Make it look visually good
Use style, be creative

#### Involve your audience
Show them how do you get in that stage, **show the drafts**!

#### Be a listener
Wait for people talk, and ask them to talk too

### Always answer
Do not let people in vacuum
