Given an architecture like a e-commerce, which is the most part of the application to grow: the sign up/in, the admin painel or the purchases? 

If you design to scale the admin painel, you might take the wrong guess because is more likely your architecture grow in purchases and sign in/ups than hidden admin painel.

**To scale well, shot on the right parameter.**
