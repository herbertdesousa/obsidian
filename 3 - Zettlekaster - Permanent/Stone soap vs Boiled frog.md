Stone soap: Deceive the villagers (stakeholders) to **slowly** achieve the soap for everybody (success)
Boiled frog: Cook **slowly** the frog (stakeholders) by **slowly** increase the temperature (tech debits/complexity)

### How to know it?

**Objective - Who wins promoting this proposal (fixing this broken window, refactoring, architecture issue...)**
My <u>ego</u> or <u>hidden incompetence</u>? This is **Boiled Flog** 
Related: [[Software Entropy - Broken windows vs Loose big picture]]

**Subjective - Can you reveal the proposal?**
If it takes <u>more than 2 weeks</u>, <u>more than 1 sprint</u>... This is **Boiled Flog**
